About EMBR Entertainment

Launched in 2023, EMBR Entertainment is one of the earliest vertical mini-drama studios in the U.S., producing films for ReelShort, MyDrama, and FlexTV. Across major platform releases, EMBR titles have generated nearly 1 billion total views.

EMBR Milestones

Mr. Williams, Madame is Dying generated $10 million for FlexTV in six weeks, remains the platform's highest-ranked and most-viewed title with nearly 200 million views, and won Best Overseas Drama at the 2024 International Short Drama Festival.

My Sister is the Warlord Queen surpassed 270 million views on ReelShort in under 90 days.

Ex-Convict Nanny and Billionaire Single Dad exceeded 200 million views on ReelShort in under 120 days.

Love Score, produced from an EMBR-developed script, earned a strong review from Vertical Drama Love and became one of MyDrama's most-viewed films.

Falling In Love with My Ex-Husband Again reached #9 on DreameShort's Best of 2024 list, while Truth or Dare climbed to #4 most-viewed on SaltyTV.

Chris Wicke

Chris Wicke is founder and executive producer of EMBR Entertainment and a 25-year entertainment industry veteran. He has produced content for Discovery, CNN, MTV, Samsung, and other global networks and brands.

As one of the first American producers to embrace the vertical mini-drama format, he has produced 19 mini dramas and continues to oversee development and production for ReelShort, MyDrama/Fox Entertainment, DramaBox, and FlexTV.
